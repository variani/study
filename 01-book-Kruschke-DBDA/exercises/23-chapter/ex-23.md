### Part A


```r
ordinal_prob <- function(mu = 3.5, sigma = 2.0,
  thresh = c(1.5, 2.5, 3.5, 4.5, 5.5, 6.5))
{
  pToThresh <- pnorm(thresh, mu, sigma)
  c(pToThresh, 1) - c(0, pToThresh)
}  

# Figure 23.1 (1)
```
